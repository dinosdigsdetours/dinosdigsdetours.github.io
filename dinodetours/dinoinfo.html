Basic Information

Dino Detours is meant to be an easy to use, fun and educational site. The maps show the locations where fossils have been found. The links show images and descriptions of those fossils while the collections link directs you to the scientific database with more technical information. The dates are rough estimates. We try to be scientifically accurate, but don't guarantee this and sometimes choose clarity and simplicity over scientific precision.

Dinosaurs and Friends show: dinosauria, pterosauria, ichthyosauria, plesiosauria, mosasaurus, crocodylomorpha.

Young mammals show mammalia from 30 million to 1 million years ago.

Old mammals are mammalia older than 30 million years.

Trilobites are trilobita.
